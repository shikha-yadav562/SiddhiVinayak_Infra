Introduction

About Siddhivinayak Infra

Siddhivinayak Infra Logistics is a premier organization executing a vast array of projects including Piling Construction, Railway Infrastructure, Civil Work, and Heavy Logistics. We have proven expertise in executing complex engineering and logistical tasks for top-tier government and private clients.

Aims & Objectives

  • ? To deliver high-quality, time-bound construction and logistics solutions across India.
  • ? To maintain the highest standards of safety and structural integrity in all piling and civil works.
  • ? To seamlessly bridge the gap between heavy infrastructure requirements and logistical execution.

Four Core Divisions

DIVISION 02

Railway Infrastructure

Specialized capabilities in supporting major railway infrastructure projects across India. We handle precision Sleeper Transportation, track material logistics, and large-scale railway construction support, ensuring robust and timely execution for critical national networks.

DIVISION 03

RMC & Stone Supply

Providing continuous and reliable supply chains for construction materials. We supply high-grade aggregate, JCB stones, and manage Ready-Mix Concrete (RMC) logistics to keep massive construction sites operating without material shortages.

DIVISION 04

Logistics & Our Fleet

Our massive in-house fleet powers all our operations. From ODC (Over Dimensional Cargo) trucks to heavy-duty trailers and cranes, our logistics division ensures machinery, infrastructure components, and construction materials reach anywhere in India securely.
